About Me

Hello!! I'm Apon D'Cruze.
Currently, I’m a Computer Science and Engineering student at the University of Asia Pacific. While my academic focus is rooted in technology, I have a deep passion for reading and writing fiction. I’ve participated in several fictional writing competitions, including one organized by Bangladesh Art Nation, where I ranked in the top 30 nationwide.
In addition to my academic and literary pursuits, I’ve gained professional experience as a Customer Service Officer at Uber BD. My diverse interests also extend to broadcasting, as I aspire to become a Radio Jockey one day. Moreover, I have a keen interest in photography and aim to pursue it professionally in the future.
